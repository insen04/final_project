Artist Statement

First, the questions I think of are, “how can I design a product that is both aesthetically pleasing and functional?” To create a product, I believe that the most part is to know what the user’s needs and that the product is useful. Secondly, “how can I change the daily life objects into a unique one to make mundane environment into an entertaining space?” From the past two years, I noticed that I want to see objects from a different point of view. Also, I want to have a positive impact on the users to have fun while interacting with the product. The question “how can I find the relationship between art and technology?” made me to always to adapt to a rapidly changing technologies and to create a “new” art piece.
